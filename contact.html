Common Questions

FAQ

How do I purchase an artwork?

Click "Inquire to Purchase" on any artwork in our gallery, or send us a message via the contact form above. Our advisors will guide you through pricing, shipping, and payment options.

Do you offer worldwide shipping?

Yes — we ship to over 60 countries. All works are professionally packaged and fully insured in transit. Shipping costs and timelines are confirmed at the time of purchase.

Can I commission a bespoke artwork?

Absolutely. Select "Commission Request" in the contact form and describe your vision, budget, and timeline. We'll match you with an artist from our roster who best fits your brief.

How do I apply to exhibit my work?

We review artist submissions on a rolling basis. Send us your portfolio — 10–15 images, artist statement, and CV — using the contact form with subject "Artist Submission".

What are the benefits of Lumina Membership?

Members enjoy free unlimited gallery access, invitations to private openings and previews, discounts on art purchases, and a quarterly curator's letter with curated recommendations.

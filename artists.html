Abstract · Expressionism · Oil Painting

Isabelle Moreau

Born in Paris, France, Isabelle Moreau is one of the most celebrated abstract expressionists of her generation. Her work draws from the emotional undercurrents of everyday life — grief, elation, longing — and transforms them into swirling, layered canvases that feel almost alive. Her pieces have been shown in over 30 international galleries and are held in private collections across Europe and North America.

Geometric · Contemporary · Mixed Media

Marco DeLuca

A trained architect from Rome, Marco DeLuca channels structural philosophy into striking visual art. His canvases pit sharp geometric forms against one another in high-contrast colour fields, exploring themes of conflict, tension, and balance. DeLuca has been shortlisted for the Turner Prize and his installations have graced the Venice Biennale twice, cementing his place in the contemporary art canon.
